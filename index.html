LQCD SciDAC-4 Project

The Lattice Quantum Chromo-Dynamics (LQCD) SciDAC-4 Project is supported by the U.S. Dept. of Energy Office of Nuclear Physics and the Office of Advanced Scientific Computing Research. The project is supporting the development software infrastructure to carry out scientific calculations in support of the U.S. Nuclear Physics program. It is a collaboration of domain scientists, and computer scientists which aims to understand the structure of matter under extreme conditions, and how quarks and gluons are confined to build the matter that is observed in nature.
