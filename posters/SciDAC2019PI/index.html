Accelerating Gauge Generation for Lattice QCD on Summit
Authors: Kate Clark (NVIDIA Corporation), Bálint Joó (Jefferson Lab), Wei Sun (Jefferson Lab), Mathias Wagner (NVIDIA Corporation), Frank Winter (Jefferson Lab), Evan Weinberg (NVIDIA Corporation)
Poster Files: Keynote PDF
Contact: Bálint Joó email: bjoo at jlab.org
Abstract:

Following the successful incorporation of the QUDA Adaptive Multi-Grid solver into Chroma, which yielded an 82x integrated speedup on Summit since this work began, we discuss continuing algorithmic and development work in currently in progress to stabilize and speed up our gauge generation calculations

MG Proto: A Multigrid Solver for x86 multicore Systems
Authors: Bálint Joó (Jefferson Lab), Thorsten Kurth (NERSC)
Poster Files: Keynote PDF
Contact: Bálint Joó email: bjoo at jlab.org
Abstract:

We present the status of MG Proto, a prototype (but production capable) multi-grid solver for Wilson Clover fermions, designed to run on Intel Xeon Phi, Knights Landing and general x86 multi-core systems. We discuss details of threading, vectorization and present performance on the Stampede 2 Skylake System.
